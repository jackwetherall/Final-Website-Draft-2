Scope & Strategy plane

My plan behind my html website was to create a modern, stylish, and minimalistic website that is easy to navigate for everyone, my main target audience being people in an age range similar to myself. Despite this, it is easy to navigate and suitable for all age ranges as my parents and aunts and uncles will want to see my projects from this first semester at university. On top of this Mary and Mic will be marking my work so I would like to make it suitable for them. My functional goals for this website are to create a user friendly and appealing navigation bar with animations for this and for the pictures of my work. In my website I will not only be including design work from graphic and web design, but I intend to include photos of my time here at Bristol so far as backgrounds of my website that captures a new and exciting life that I have began here. I have chosen the photos carefully to create a specific feel and energy that I feel captures an insight into the student life here in Bristol outside of our studies in such a unique, creative and expressive city. The actual functions my website needs to carry out is have a working navigation system that links to the other pages of my website. It needs to also hyperlink screenshots of my coding worksheets to my GitHub On top of this, my website also needs to be suitable for smaller screen sizes such as small laptops as family and friends who would be viewing my page could potentially be using small laptops.
