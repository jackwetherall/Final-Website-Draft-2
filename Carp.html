Carp Task

Here are the True/False Festival posters showcasing more of my design work.

For this poster I created a repeating marbled background effect using photoshop, and to suit this style of a swirled marble I warped the text into a shape I liked. I then used repetition by putting the text behind itself, lowering the oppacity of it each time. I used contrast by putting a drop shadow behind the text in black. For allignment I alligned the date and website in the top right and left corners. I have used proximity by ensuring everything is arranged evenly and appears professional and not clumped together. I chose a red white and black colour scheme to suit the logo.

For this poster I used a bold typeface & repeated the text, lowering the thickness of the text stroke line by line focusing attention to the center text. Then for the other text I followed this style by repeating the text a layer behind itself. I alligned these evenly by creating & following guidelines, slanting the text to the right. For this poster I also used a red & white colour scheme. I have used proximity by putting the date in the top left, and the website link in the bottom right which creates enough space in the poster. For a clear contrast I have used a red backdrop with white bold center text. to folow the colour of the logo.

For this design I created a retro pacman themed poster. I used letters that were shared between words to link them into one centered peice of text. I then incorperated pac-man characters into the text to add more depth to the center font. To recreate other parts of the game I created a box for the ghosts with text required to be included in the poster, and added a "Score" indicator in the top left of the poster.
